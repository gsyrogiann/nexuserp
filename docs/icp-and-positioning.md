# NexusERP ICP and Positioning Baseline

## Goal

Define a practical first-market position for NexusERP so pilot selection,
product prioritization, and commercial messaging stay focused.

## Recommended Initial ICP

The recommended initial Ideal Customer Profile for NexusERP v1.0 is:

* Greek small-to-medium commercial or service businesses
* teams that need invoicing, customer management, and operational follow-up in one system
* organizations with lightweight ERP/CRM needs rather than heavy enterprise customization
* teams that benefit from Gmail, telephony, and AI-assisted operational workflows

## Best-Fit Customer Characteristics

Prioritize customers that have:

* 3 to 50 internal users
* active customer and supplier management
* recurring invoice and reporting needs
* willingness to adopt process changes during a pilot
* low tolerance for fragmented spreadsheets and disconnected tools

## Strong Use Cases

NexusERP is currently best positioned for customers who need:

* customer and supplier directory management
* invoice workflow and reporting visibility
* Gmail-linked customer communication tracking
* VoIP-linked call logging and AI summaries
* early-stage operational visibility without enterprise-grade overhead

## Avoid as First Commercial Targets

Avoid leading with customers that require:

* deep custom workflows before pilot validation
* hard contractual uptime guarantees before operational maturity
* certified tax or compliance claims beyond current readiness
* large multi-country or multi-entity accounting complexity
* immediate on-prem delivery before deployment model decisions are finalized

## Core Value Proposition Draft

NexusERP helps growing Greek businesses run invoicing, customer operations, and
communication workflows from one practical operational system, while reducing
manual follow-up through AI-assisted Gmail and VoIP workflows.

## Positioning Constraints

Do not position NexusERP yet as:

* a fully mature enterprise ERP
* a compliance-certified accounting suite
* a fully proven omni-channel communication platform
* a finished AI automation product with zero human oversight

## Sales Qualification Questions

Use these questions before accepting a pilot:

* Is invoicing one of the customer's daily operational pains?
* Do they have a real customer and supplier workflow to validate?
* Will they actually use Gmail or VoIP integration if enabled?
* Can they provide a responsive pilot owner for weekly feedback?
* Is their scope close enough to the current product to generate learning quickly?

## Immediate Next Decision

Before broader selling begins, confirm whether NexusERP will launch first as:

* operational SaaS for Greek SMEs
* verticalized CRM-plus-invoicing workflow product
* integration-first pilot platform for selected early adopters
